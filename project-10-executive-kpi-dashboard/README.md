# Project 10 – Executive KPI Dashboard

Enterprise Executive KPI Dashboard using Grafana, Prometheus, Business Intelligence Metrics, and Executive Reporting.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates executive-level KPI monitoring and strategic business visibility.

The dashboard provides real-time visibility into revenue performance, customer growth, financial metrics, operational efficiency, and strategic objectives.

The solution is designed for:

- CEOs
- CIOs
- CTOs
- Executive Leadership Teams
- Business Managers
- PMO Directors
- Digital Transformation Leaders

---

# Business Scenario

Executives require accurate business intelligence to make strategic decisions.

Without centralized KPI monitoring organizations may experience:

- Delayed Decision Making
- Lack of Business Visibility
- Revenue Tracking Issues
- Poor Operational Insight
- Strategic Misalignment

This dashboard provides a centralized executive reporting platform.
