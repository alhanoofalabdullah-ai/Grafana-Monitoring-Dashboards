# Project 18 – Business KPI Monitoring Dashboard

Enterprise Business KPI Monitoring Dashboard using Grafana, Prometheus, Executive Scorecards, and Business Analytics.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates enterprise-grade KPI monitoring and business performance management.

The dashboard provides real-time visibility into revenue performance, customer growth, financial performance, operational efficiency, and strategic business objectives.

The solution is designed for:

- CEOs
- CIOs
- CFOs
- PMO Directors
- Department Managers
- Business Analysts
- Executive Leadership Teams

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations require continuous monitoring of key business indicators to ensure strategic success.

Without KPI visibility organizations may experience:

- Delayed Decision Making
- Poor Performance Tracking
- Revenue Loss
- Customer Retention Issues
- Strategic Misalignment
- Reduced Operational Efficiency

This dashboard provides centralized KPI tracking and executive reporting.
