# Project 16 – Business Intelligence Dashboard

Enterprise Business Intelligence Dashboard using Grafana, Prometheus, Business Analytics, and Executive Reporting.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates enterprise-grade Business Intelligence (BI) monitoring and executive analytics.

The dashboard provides real-time visibility into revenue performance, customer behavior, financial health, operational efficiency, and strategic business insights.

The solution is designed for:

- Executive Leadership
- CEOs
- CIOs
- CFOs
- Business Analysts
- PMO Directors
- Digital Transformation Leaders

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations require accurate business intelligence to make data-driven decisions.

Without BI visibility organizations may experience:

- Poor Decision Making
- Revenue Leakage
- Customer Churn
- Operational Inefficiencies
- Limited Strategic Visibility
- Delayed Executive Reporting

This dashboard provides centralized business analytics and executive insights.
